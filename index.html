Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting started.

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes! Our platform is fully free for learners — no hidden fees or subscriptions required.
3. Do I need to create an account?
You’re welcome to explore and learn without an account! But if you create one, you’ll get a more personalized and improved experience.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
Our platform helps you improve your vocabulary by providing words with clear meanings and related synonyms to strengthen your understanding.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Currently, we do not offer certificates for completed courses. However, we wish you the very best in your learning journey with our website.
